Just a Touch

Short film, 2017

In 2017, I collaborated with another researcher, an animation studio, and a sound designer from Italy to creat a short, animated film about the neuroscience of touch perception. My role included co-creating the film concept, co-writing the script, co-consulting on the science, and narration. The film won the best National/International Film at the Bristol Science Film Festival (2017), and was officially selected for the Goethe Institut Science Film Festival, and the Super Geek Film Festival.
