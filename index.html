Hello, I'm Lev, a behavioural scientist with an academic background in cognitive psychology and neuroscience. My current work is focused on augmenting human agency by using Generative AI to help people think more clearly and act more intentionally, particularly in workplace collaboration. I'm also exploring the broader impacts of Generative AI on cognition, people, and workflows. I work in the Tools for Thought team at Microsoft Research in Cambridge UK.

Previously, I worked as a Research Advisor at the Behavioural Insights Team, where I specialised in the design, implementation, and analysis of experiments across health, social care, and education. For example, I worked on a successful national-scale field RCT to increase COVID-19 vaccinations in a cohort of ~2.3 million people.

I have a PhD in neuroscience from the University of Oxford, and have also previously dabbled in science communication and policy research.

I am most excited by the intersection of behavioural science, technology, design, and social innovation. Use the list below to explore my past projects. Email me at lev.tankelevitch@gmail.com, or find me on Twitter/X, Bluesky, or LinkedIn.
